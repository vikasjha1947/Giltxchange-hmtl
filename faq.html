Cryptocurrency Withdrawals

Miner fee.**

**Giltxchange will set mining fees for withdrawals. These mining fees are subject to change without notice due to network volatility. These fees will be held in a miner fee reserve pool. Giltxchange will group user withdrawals and send withdrawals to the blockchain together in the next available block. This is so Giltxchange can keep fees as low as possible while having fast withdrawals. In rare cases, the blockchain explorer will show a fee lower or higher than what was set by Giltxchange. In these cases, the remainder will return to the miner fee reserve pool, or the miner fee reserve pool will cover the higher fee.
